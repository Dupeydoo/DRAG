About DRAG

DRAG is all about generating and diversifying drum music. In the past, musicians have tirelessly put pen to paper in order to produce accompaniment to their music. DRAG simplifies this process by using an Interactive Genetic Algorithm to manipulate a drum track which you input.

Once you have rated tracks for a number of manual generations, machine learning is used to automatically learn a function representing how you learn tracks. Thus, DRAG automates hundreds of generations for you instantly, before reporting back with the final generation.

DRAG works on drum tracks that consist of two measures at 4:4. 8 beats per bar is DRAG's language. Although simple, it is hoped that you can utilise DRAG to either produce accompaniment you may have not thought of, or help the novice generate accompaniment for their projects.

DRAG is the work of a dissertation on Interactive Genetic Algorithms and music produced by James Du Plessis. I hope that the tool is both useful and provides you with the fun I intended when building it.
